## Notes on Reliability

- The Comprehensive Rules PDFs are treated as primary authority for rule engine behavior.
- The official Animation page is treated as primary authority for anime/season naming, not for mechanical rules.
- The Card List/Card Search pages are treated as primary authority for individual card text, card numbers, clans/nations, and expansion membership.
- Markdown files in `../vanguard_rules_markdown/versions/` are text extractions. They preserve PDF URL and SHA-256, but PDF icon glyphs and diagram-only details may need verification against the original PDF.
